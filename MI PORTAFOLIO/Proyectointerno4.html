Delta Air Lines × Safety Campaign

STAY TOGETHER

01

The Insight

Every year, thousands of children get lost in international airports, creating stressful and dangerous situations for families. Delta Air Lines recognized this tension and decided to act.

02

The Concept

Through thoughtful design and simple technology, the “Stay Together” campaign proposes a solution that helps families stay connected and safe while traveling.

Protecting and caring for passengers during their journey.

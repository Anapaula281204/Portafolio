DUOLINGO: DUO LOVE POTION

The Concept

Our campaign for Duolingo, called “Duo Love Potion,” focuses on couples in long-distance relationships who speak different languages or come from different cultures.

The idea behind the campaign is that language can be the key to strengthening love and connection, even when people are physically far apart.

The concept of the “love potion” is used as a metaphor: learning your partner’s language becomes the magical ingredient that helps couples communicate better.

Meaningful Experience

Emotional Connection

Through this campaign, we present Duolingo as the tool that helps turn language learning into an emotional experience. Instead of seeing it only as an educational app, the campaign highlights how learning a language keeps love strong across borders.

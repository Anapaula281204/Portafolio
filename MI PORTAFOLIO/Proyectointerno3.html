Before the Show

This photography project is about dance and the emotions dancers feel just before going on stage.

I love dancing, and through this project I wanted to capture those moments of nervousness, excitement, and concentration before a performance. Dance is a form of art that can express many things without using words.

Through movement, body language, and emotion, dancers can communicate powerful feelings. This project explores those quiet but intense moments before the show, highlighting the beauty and emotion that the art of dance transmits.
